Who We Are

An initiative built by first-gen lawyers, for the lawyers coming next.

Genvolegal is an initiative by first-gen lawyers to ease the professional difficulties faced by upcoming legal professionals. To smooth their career path, Genvolegal bridges gaps in theory-to-practice training and industrial exposure that lopsided access to mentorship creates.

What We Do

We believe in learning by doing.

As we believe in learning by doing, we organise comprehensive hands-on training sessions and workshops to equip law students and young legal professionals with the skills required to sustain themselves in the legal field.
